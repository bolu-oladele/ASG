FAQs

Answers to the most frequently asked questions.

What types of events do you specialise in?

↓

We specialize in a wide range of events, including weddings, birthday parties, corporate events, anniversaries and themed parties. Our team can bring creativity and expertise to and any event you envision.

Can you help with both small and large events?

↓

Yes, we handle events of all sizes! Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large celebration, out team will work with you to create a memorable experience tailored to your needs.

Do you offer customizable event packages?

↓

Absolutely! We provide customizable event packages to fit your budget and preferences. From full-service planning to a la carte options, we ensure your event aligns with your vision and needs.

How far in advance should I book your services?

↓

We recommend booking our services as early as possible, especially for larger events, to ensure availability and sufficient time for planning. However, we are also equipped to handle short-notice events when needed.
